---

## Workflow Explanation

### Step 1
Video is captured from CCTV cameras or uploaded by the user.

### Step 2
The AI module detects people using YOLO.

### Step 3
The system counts people and estimates crowd density.

### Step 4
Risk analysis is performed.

### Step 5
The backend processes the results.

### Step 6
The frontend displays the crowd statistics and alerts.

### Step 7
Authorities receive notifications if the crowd becomes dangerous.
